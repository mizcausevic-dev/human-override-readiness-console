# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-back

This repo demonstrates the **human override readiness** primitive — supervisor coverage, intervention drills, telemetry continuity, handoff packets, and operator training posture.

For Kinetic Gain Embedded, that matters because robotics and industrial autonomy products often need:

- override-safe fleet summaries
- evidence-ready intervention history
- operator-safe review surfaces
- low-risk analytics hooks inside the product without turning the app into a raw control-room clone

Embedded extends this into in-product autonomy review, escalation-safe telemetry, and operational analytics layers that remain safe, observable, and revenue-relevant.
